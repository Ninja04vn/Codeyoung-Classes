Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974),[1] known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema.[2][3] He is among the highest paid actors in India.[4][5][6][7][8][9] He has played the lead in 66 films and the International Business Times framed him as a "consistent performer".[10] Referred to as "Thalapathy", Vijay has a significant following internationally.[4][5] He has won several awards, including a South Indian International Movie Award.[11] Early life and family Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar in a Tamil speaking family on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu. His father S. A. Chandrasekhar is a Tamil film director[12] and his mother Shoba Chandrasekhar is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.[13] His father is of Christian descent and his mother is Hindu.[14][15] Vijay was baptised at the age of 12.[14] Vijay had a sister, Vidhya, who died when she was two years old.[16][17] Initially attending Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School[18] at Kodambakkam, Vijay later joined the Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School[19] at Virugambakkam and went on to pursue a degree in Visual Communications from Loyola College. While studying, at the age of 18, he asked his father if "he would launch him".[20]
